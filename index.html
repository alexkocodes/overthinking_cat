This is Alex. Alex is one of the cats on campus. Alex loves sleeping on the staircase and blocking people's way. But recently, he has been really distracted because he has a crush on...

Taylor

Taylor is a Golden British Shorthair who is popular among the students. Rumor has it that Taylor grooms themselves 20 times a day to keep their fur clean and shiny.

Alex has been thinking about this for a while... but today he is going to ask Taylor out!

How is Alex feeling?

>Alex pees to mark his territory. Taylor will love his smell!

Alex collects the best (freshest!) gifts in town for Taylor.

Alex puts on his best suit. There's no way Taylor could resist him!

In his head, prepared and ready to go, Alex finally asks Taylor out. Taylor is blown away by how amazing Alex is and immeadiately falls in love with him. "Yes! Of course! I've been waiting for you to ask me out!"

In his head, they live happily ever after...

Back to reality...

Alex is too scared to approach Taylor. He hides behind the bushes instead.

Alex decided to eat all the chocolates he bought for Taylor by himself.

Disclaimer: chocolate gives cats extra heartache. Do not try this at home!

Alex doesn't think he's good enough for Taylor.

In his head Taylor rejects Alex INFRONT of other cats, and Alex could not be more embarrassed. "Ew, no!" replied Taylor, breaking Alex's heart forever.

Back to reality...

In reality, when Alex asks Taylor out, they agree to go on a first date. He's surprised at how much he overthought it.

"Huh? That was it? Nothing more?" Confident Alex thought.

"Huh? That was it? That went better than I thought!" Confident Alex thought.

Sometimes…

overthinking is like taking unnecessary turns instead of a straight line to your final destination...
